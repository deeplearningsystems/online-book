    Deep Learning Systems

    Deep Learning Systems: Algorithms, Compilers, Processors, Platforms, and Data Centers for Efficient Model Training and Deployment

    Andres Rodriguez

    The explosive growth in Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the infrastructure of data centers. This book describes the state-of-the-art AI models deployed across top companies, explains the techniques to efficiently train and deply these models at scale, and details the AI-centric data center infrastructure from processor components to multi-rack systems and from compilers to high-level software libraries and tool.
